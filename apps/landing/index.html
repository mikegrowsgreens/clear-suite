AI-Powered Lead Enrichment Platform

Shipday (Delivery SaaS)

Built an automated lead scoring system that enriches restaurant prospects with delivery volume estimates, POS system detection, and pain point analysis in under 5 seconds.

View Full Case Study ▼

The Problem

Sales team spending 20-30 minutes per lead manually researching restaurants: checking Google reviews, estimating delivery volume, identifying POS systems, and determining if they were good fit. This research bottleneck meant reps could only qualify 10-15 leads per day. Many high-value prospects were ignored because initial data was incomplete.

What I Built

Automated lead enrichment platform using Make.com workflows integrated with Claude API, Google Maps API, and multiple data sources:

  • Data Collection: Webhook triggers pull new leads from Google Sheets, automatically scraping website, Google Maps profile, and POS system indicators
  • AI Analysis: Claude Opus analyzes business maturity, estimates weekly delivery volume, identifies current delivery management method, and detects operational pain points
  • Lead Scoring: Multi-factor algorithm scores leads 0-150 based on cuisine type, delivery volume, third-party platform dependency, location count, and business age
  • Smart Routing: Automatically assigns high-scoring leads (80+) to senior reps via Slack, schedules demos for medium-scoring leads (50-79), and nurtures low-scoring leads via email sequences
  • Output: Enriched profiles include specific pain points, estimated deal size, recommended pitch angles, and competitor analysis
Make.com Claude Opus 4 Google Maps API Google Sheets Slack API Web Scraping

Business Impact

  • 95% time reduction in lead qualification (30 minutes → 5 seconds automated)
  • 4x increase in daily lead qualification capacity per rep
  • Higher win rates on scored leads vs. unscored (tracking in progress)
  • Better territory planning enabled by enriched location data
  • Sales team now reviews 10 pre-qualified, enriched leads each morning vs. manually researching cold lists

Technical Architecture

Multi-stage workflow processes 100+ leads per day: (1) Webhook receives new lead → (2) Google Maps API enriches with rating, review count, phone, website → (3) Web scraper extracts POS system from website footer → (4) Claude analyzes all data and returns structured JSON with scores, pain points, and recommendations → (5) Make.com router distributes based on score → (6) Updates Google Sheets with enriched data → (7) Sends Slack notifications to assigned reps with summary and pitch angles.

Automated Upsell Campaign Engine

GigFinesse (Gig Worker SaaS)

Designed and deployed multi-touch email campaigns that automatically promoted premium tier upgrades to qualified free and basic plan users, recovering 15%+ of monthly revenue.

View Full Case Study ▼

The Problem

Account Executives had no systematic way to identify upsell opportunities or reach out to existing customers. Valuable expansion revenue was being left on the table because reps were 100% focused on new business. During winter months (typically slow season in gig economy), pipeline dried up and revenue dipped. Company needed a way to maintain consistent revenue growth without hiring more AEs.

What I Built

Fully automated upsell campaign system that identified upgrade opportunities and executed multi-touch outreach:

  • Customer Segmentation: Python scripts analyzed usage data to identify high-engagement free users and basic plan power users showing premium feature interest
  • Trigger Logic: Monitored specific behaviors (frequency of logins, feature exploration patterns, support ticket themes) to determine ideal upgrade timing
  • Email Sequences: 5-email drip campaigns with A/B tested messaging, case study CTAs, and limited-time offers
  • Winter Campaign Specialization: Created season-specific messaging highlighting how premium features solve slow-season challenges
  • Performance Tracking: Dashboard showing campaign opens, clicks, upgrade conversions, and revenue attribution
Python Make.com Google Sheets Email API Data Analysis

Business Impact

  • 15%+ of monthly closed revenue came from automated campaigns
  • Higher conversion rates during winter months (traditionally slower selling season in gig economy)
  • Mitigated normal sales valley by maintaining consistent upsell motion year-round
  • Freed account team to focus on new business and strategic expansions

What I Learned

Simple automation executed consistently beats complex systems that need constant management. The key was segmentation accuracy and letting the data trigger the right message at the right time. Winter campaign timing proved critical - while competitors slowed outreach during slow season, automated campaigns maintained pipeline velocity and converted higher because prospects had more time to evaluate.

AI Discovery Call Preparation System

Personal System (Used at Multiple Companies)

Created an AI-powered research assistant that generates comprehensive discovery call prep documents in 15 minutes, including company background, question funnels, and objection handling.

View Full Case Study ▼

The Problem

Effective discovery calls require 30-60 minutes of prep work: researching the company, understanding their industry challenges, preparing relevant questions, anticipating objections, and having supporting resources ready. Most reps skip this work and wing it, leading to generic conversations and lower close rates. Even when done properly, this manual research doesn't scale.

What I Built

Automated discovery prep system that generates call-ready documentation:

  • Company Intelligence: AI researches prospect's website, LinkedIn, recent news, and industry context
  • Question Funnels: Generates 3-4 question paths organized by pain point category, with follow-up prompts and credibility builders
  • Opening Script: Creates personalized intro that demonstrates research and establishes point-of-view
  • Objection Preparation: Predicts likely objections based on company profile and industry, provides response frameworks
  • Resource Matching: Suggests relevant case studies, ROI calculators, and proof points based on prospect's profile
  • Next Steps: Recommends ideal call-to-action and follow-up strategy

Business Impact

  • 75% time reduction in discovery prep (60 minutes → 15 minutes)
  • Consistent preparation quality across all reps regardless of experience level
  • More confident calls with prepared responses to likely objections
  • Better conversation control using structured question funnels
  • New reps ramp faster by learning what good discovery prep looks like

Technical Architecture

Built using ChatGPT Projects with custom system prompts and structured output formatting. Prospect info (company name, website, LinkedIn, vertical) is input → GPT researches and analyzes → Returns formatted document with all prep components → Rep reviews and adjusts based on specific call context. System is reusable across any B2B sales role and has been personally used at Toast, Dutchie, Shipday, and GigFinesse.
